About

I’m interested in unsupervised learning for multivariate time-series and functional data, especially from wearable sensors. My current work explores clustering methods and representation learning to make sense of long-horizon physiological signals.

Projects & Research

  • Cluster Analysis of Multi-Variate Functional Data via Non-linear Representations — methods for grouping long-form sensor signals (heart rate, activity, etc.) modeled as continuous functions of time.
  • Tooling for Wearable Data Pipelines — cleaning, windowing, and feature extraction for noisy on-body sensors.
